About Us

Since 2021, we have spread we are a group of high schoolers based in Connecticut who are passionate about the world of STEM. We aim to promote interest in STEM not only within our state but throughout the US as a whole by hosting science competitions, fairs, conventions, and more. Our inspiration comes from the love of STEM. We experience when working together to prepare for the STEM competitions we compete in as a group.

Our mission is to provide a better scientific foundation for all, especially high school students who are passionate about STEM. The goals of our nonprofit, the events we host, and the outreach which we do circulate around one idea, and that is providing a better scientific foundation for all. Some of the events hosted by the Gamma Group include the Summer Science Bowl Tournament, also known as SSBT which is a national-based competition taken through the online platform, Discord. Through these competitions, the Gamma Group hopes that it can spark greater interest and passion for STEM and STEM competitions. In addition, the Gamma Group hopes through their partnering schools and companies, that they can build a community of students all across the nation who are passionate about Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math!
