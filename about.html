FAQs

What are SSBT & WSBT?

The Summer/Winter Science Bowl Tournament is a national level competition that is hosted by a student-led organization called The Gamma Group. The tournament is open to high school students from all over the United States and is held in a virtual setting. The tournament follows the same rules as the National Science Bowl®, which is a competition sponsored by the U.S. Department of Energy. The tournament aims to promote interest and excellence in science, technology, engineering and mathematics among students.

What is the cost of participation?

The questions are written by experienced past and current National Science Bowl high school competitors who are part of the Gamma Group and are not released to anyone outside of the moderators until the competition is over. The tournament does not have any fees or entrance prices, so anyone can participate without worrying about the cost.
